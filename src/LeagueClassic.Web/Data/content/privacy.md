# Privacy Policy

*Effective date: July 2026*

League Classic Archive ("we," "us") runs this site at leagueclassicarchive.net. This page explains what information we collect, why, and what you can do about it. We've tried to write it in plain language rather than legalese.

## What we collect

**Account information.** When you register, we collect your email address, a password (stored as a salted hash — we never see or store your actual password), and the display name you choose. Your email is used for login and account recovery only; it's never shown publicly.

**Content you post.** Guides, build details, forum threads and replies, comments, and votes you cast are stored against your account so we can display them and let you edit or delete your own work later.

**Moderation reports.** If you report content, we store the report (reason, optional details, who filed it) so moderators can review it.

**Technical data.** Like any web service, our hosting infrastructure processes IP addresses to serve pages and to rate-limit posting (this is how we blunt spam floods) — this isn't stored in our database, though it may appear transiently in standard server logs.

## What we don't do

- We don't run ads, trackers, or analytics on this site today. If that changes, we'll update this policy first and add the consent controls that go with it (particularly for visitors in the EU/UK).
- We don't sell or rent your information to anyone, ever.
- We don't email you anything except account-related messages (like password resets). No newsletters, no marketing.

## Cookies

We use two kinds of cookies, both strictly functional:

- An **authentication cookie**, so you stay logged in between visits.
- An **anti-forgery cookie**, a security measure that protects your account from a class of attack called cross-site request forgery.

Neither is used for tracking or advertising. You can block cookies in your browser, but you won't be able to log in or post if you do.

## Who else sees this data

- **Amazon Web Services** hosts our server and database.
- **Amazon SES** delivers account emails (like password resets) on our behalf.

Neither is permitted to use your data for their own purposes — they process it only to provide the hosting/email service to us.

## How long we keep it

We keep your account and content for as long as your account exists. Deleting a guide, post, or comment removes it immediately. If you want your account itself deleted, contact us (below) and we'll remove your account and personal information, though content you've contributed to shared discussions (like forum replies) may be retained in anonymized form to keep those threads coherent for other readers.

## Children's privacy

This site is not directed at, and registration is not intended for, children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we'll remove it.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change what we collect or how we use it, we'll update this page and change the effective date above.
