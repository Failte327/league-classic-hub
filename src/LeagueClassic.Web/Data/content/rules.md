# Community Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to **LeagueClassicGuides.net** — a haven built *for* gamers, *by* gamers. These rules exist for one reason: to keep this a place everyone is glad to log into. Read them once, play nice, and we'll all get along just fine.

## The Golden Rule

Treat every Summoner the way you'd want to be treated. Behind every username is a real person who loves this game as much as you do. Disagree all you like — passionately, even — but keep it about the ideas, never the person.

## 1. Keep it respectful

- No harassment, personal attacks, or targeted hostility.
- No hate speech or slurs of any kind — zero tolerance, no exceptions.
- Flaming, trolling, and baiting have no place here. Save the tilt for solo queue.

## 2. Keep it clean

This is an all-ages community. Keep language, images, and links safe for younger Summoners and workplaces alike. No explicit, graphic, or NSFW content.

## 3. Stay on topic

- Post in the board that fits your topic — it helps everyone find things.
- Search before starting a new thread; there may already be a great discussion going.
- Keep guides useful and honest. Share what works, explain your reasoning, and credit others when you build on their ideas.

## 4. No spam or exploitation

- No spam, chain posts, or repetitive flooding.
- No advertising, referral links, or self-promotion unrelated to League Classic.
- No scams, phishing, account sales, or anything that puts fellow Summoners at risk.

## 5. Play fair with content

- Post your own work, or content you have permission to share.
- Don't repost someone else's guide as your own — link to and credit the original author.

## 6. Respect the game and each other's fun

All builds, all champions, and all playstyles are welcome here — whether you're theory-crafting the perfect crit Yasuo or proudly maining AP Sion for the nostalgia. Let people enjoy the game their way.

## Enforcement

Moderators may edit, move, lock, or remove content and, when necessary, suspend accounts that break these rules. We aim to be fair and to talk things through first — but keeping the community healthy comes first.

Have a question, or see something that needs attention? Reach out to the moderators. We're all on the same team.
